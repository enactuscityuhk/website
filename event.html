Enactus Seed (Oct)

active members general members open to application

Enactus Seed is a one-month incubation program. Participants undergo various workshops and training provided by Enactus HK and Enactus CityU. Participants will learn the best practices of ideation, polishing, and pitching to kick start a business plan with industry leaders and interact with other young entrepreneurs. The selected two teams in the final round will have the chance to be the representatives of Enactus CityU in the Enactus World Cup Hong Kong round.

Mentorship Program (Nov - Ongoing)

active members coming Soon

The mentorship program connects members with mentors, particularly in the social entrepreneurship sector. The vigorous experience from mentors provides members with a comprehensive understanding of various industries, business environments, and company insights. The program is designed for students with a keen interest in the entrepreneurial setting or hoping to equip themselves with the transferable skills and mindset as an entrepreneur.

External Consulting Projects (Dec - May 2021)

active members coming Soon

The external consulting projects are one-of-a-kind activities at CityU. Groups of members will have the chance to run semester-long projects with social enterprises in Hong Kong as business advisors to deliver innovative ideas for improving the agencies’ performance, covering their products and marketing strategy. The members can get to know more about the operation of social enterprises in Hong Kong from an insider view and build up connections and networks with valuable industry stakeholders.

Guest Seminar on Green Business (Jan)

active members general members coming Soon

The guest seminar will introduce a relatively niche area of entrepreneurial business, which is the green business. We believe that Hong Kong has still a long way to go in developing this particular sector. The seminar hopes to bring in emerging and sustainable kinds of business models like upcycling to boost members’ ideas regarding green and sustainable business.

Entrepreneurial Forums (Jan - Mar)

active members coming Soon

The entrepreneurial forums adopt the vigorous group-learning mode through case studies. The forums provide a platform for brightest students of various backgrounds and experiences to exchange ideas regarding different latest business initiatives and social entrepreneurship cases. We aim to deliver a highly analytical, professional, and open environment for high-calibers to exchange minds and network.

Company Visit / Volunteer Work

active members general members coming Soon

The company visit/volunteer work session is a combined event that provides members both the visiting session and learn-by-doing session at one of the local social enterprises. Through this event, we hope to give the members a better insight of nature and operation into an actual social enterprise, and also make real contributions by volunteering on the spot.

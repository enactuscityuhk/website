Introduction

Enactus Seed is a compact, comprehensive one-month accelerator program designed explicitly for expediting students-initiated social innovation projects. The program consists of a series of events ranging from ideation, ideas polishing, external consultation, strategy, to prototyping. Our program welcomes social innovation ideas from a wide range of areas, covering the United Nations' 17 Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). Throughout the program, we hope to encourage students from different backgrounds to utilize their experience and knowledge to create meaningful and impactful projects which can positively impact our society.

Timeline

Internal Contests

Day 1 (17th October. 9:30 - 18:05 GMT+08)

Day1 will be an interesting and insightful day starting with the introductory speech of the president and later, will be various workshops that will help the team develop ideas and creativity. More into the day, the teams will actually get a chance to interact with the Exco-members of the committee to get their insight and interact with them. The day will end with the teams submitting a brief description of their idea or innovation.

Day 2 (18th October. 9:30 - 18:05 GMT+08)

Day 2 will also have some interactive sessions but more importantly, the teams will have to plot and express their ideas in front of a panel of judges who will score them accordingly. The shortlisted teams from the first round of pitching will then be looking towards a one-week interactive period with the exco-team where the teams will be receiving feedbacks and valuable entrepreneurial skills.

Day 3 (25th October. 9:30 - 15:30 GMT+08)

After the one-week of intensive interactive period, the teams will enter into the last and final phase of SEED where they will have to give a Quality Presentation of their product and the prospect of the product in future. Top two teams will be selected from all the shortlisted teams and will be declared as CityU Enactus SEED Winners 2020/21 and will get the opportunity to represent their product on a University Level.

Post-event Arrangements

The winning teams will be admitted to a designated network supported by both Enactus Hong Kong and Enactus CityU where they will be provided the necessary resources and expertise to further develop their ideas so as to turn the project into an social enterprise.

Prize and Rewards

The top two teams would be awarded an initial seed fund of HKD 10000 respectively to develop their products and ideas throughout the academic year. The third team will be awarded an initial seed fund of HKD 5000 as an encouragement to continue the development of the team's project.
